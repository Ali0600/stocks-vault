# SoFi Q1: record revenue +43%, EBITDA +62% (SOFI)

## Summary
A constructive (bullish) follow-up to the 2026-06-14 selloff column on [[SOFI]], arguing the
business is decoupled from the depressed stock. The routable substance — not the valuation
or rating — is the Q1 print: record GAAP net revenue $1.1B (+43% YoY), adjusted EBITDA $340M
(+62%), members 14.7M (+35%, +1.1M in the quarter), and a Q2 guide of ~$1.1B (+30%). This
complements the prior note's Technology-Platform-segment decline (the overall company grew
even as that segment slipped). Source: Obsidian Web Clip; raw clip in `_inbox/`. Headwinds
flagged: high rates pressuring lending/deposits/NIM, high marketing spend, credit risk.
