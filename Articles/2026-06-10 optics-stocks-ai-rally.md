# Optics stocks rally on the AI boom (AAOI, LITE, COHR)

## Summary
Sector-rally piece: optical component makers rose together on 2026-06-10 on
AI-datacenter optimism — [[AAOI]] led on the strength of its Q1 print and 800G
ramp, with Lumentum and Coherent (both untracked) following. The structural
anchor is NVIDIA's $4B optics initiative ($2B each into Coherent and
Lumentum). Day-move figures were written mid-session; COHR's gain had faded by
the close.
