# Situational Awareness's neocloud stakes (NBIS, CRWV)

## Summary
Profile of Leopold Aschenbrenner's AI-focused hedge fund Situational Awareness
(24-year-old ex-OpenAI researcher, "AGI by 2027" manifesto author) and its
concentration in neocloud operators: [[NBIS]] as the headline stake, [[CRWV]] as
a companion position, and [[NVDA]] framed as what the fund is rotating away from.
The piece leans on 13F-tracker data and misattributes the Nebius stake's size and
timing — the position came via a June 13G filing, not the Q1 13F (see below).
Passing mentions: OpenAI, Anthropic (both private).

## Claims & Verification
- "Nebius now comprises 38.89% or so of the portfolio" — **inaccurate** · the 13G
  filed 2026-06-10 reports 12.4M Class A shares = 5.6% of NBIS, ~$2.6B — about
  19% of the fund's $13.7B Q1 13F portfolio value (Sherwood News, 2026-06-10)
- "Huge addition [in] the first quarter" — **inaccurate** · NBIS does not appear
  in the Q1 2026 13F (holdings as of 03-31); the stake was disclosed via 13G in
  June → built after quarter-end
- "NBIS up 950%+ in past two years" — **partly verified** · +1011% vs the
  2024-10-21 Nasdaq relisting close ($20.00 → $222.24), but that's ~20 months of
  trading; NBIS didn't trade in June 2024 (yfinance)
- "Bet big money on CoreWeave" — **verified** · Q1 2026 13F shows 7.1M+ CRWV
  shares, up ~18% QoQ (Insider Monkey coverage of the filing)
- "Manages $20 billion" / "returned 1,000% since inception" — **unverified** ·
  Q1 13F portfolio value was $13.7B (up from $5.5B at end-2025); AUM and private
  fund returns aren't independently checkable
- "Shifting focus away from GPU makers toward infrastructure" — **corroborated as
  positioning** · the Q1 13F also held ~$8.46B notional chipmaker puts (incl.
  ~$1.6B vs NVDA, $2B vs the SMH ETF); 13F option lines report underlying
  notional, not premium at risk
- Context the article omits: the same Q1 13F included [[IREN]] among the fund's
  neocloud longs

## Takeaways Routed
- [[NBIS]] ← facts: 13G stake disclosure; corrected return window since relisting
- [[CRWV]] ← fact: Q1 13F position, +18% QoQ (watchlist note created)
- [[NVDA]] ← speculation: fund pairs neocloud longs with GPU-maker put exposure
- [[AI Infrastructure]] ← fact: fund's neocloud concentration; speculation:
  "compute token provider" rotation thesis
