# Bank of America's double upgrade of Intel (INTC)

## Summary
Bank of America analyst Vivek Arya issued a rare two-notch "double upgrade" on
[[INTC]] on 2026-06-11 — moving it from his most-negative to his most-positive
rating — and lifted the price target to $135 from $96. The thesis: CPUs reassert
as the coordination layer for "agentic" AI, the server-CPU market is bigger than
thought, and Intel's 18A foundry ([[Foundry process nodes]]) could become a real
second engine. The stock rose ~6.5% to ~$125 the next session. The columnist is
cautious — much may already be priced in at a rich forward multiple. Publish date
is a Saturday, so the price baseline is the 2026-06-12 (Friday) close. Passing
mentions: Apple (a hypothetical future foundry customer), Bank of America (BAC, the
upgrader) — neither routed.

## Claims & Verification
- "Double upgrade on 06-11; price target $96 → $135 (Vivek Arya, BofA)" —
  **analyst-reported** · the rating action itself, per the article

- "Stock rose ~6% to ~$125 after the upgrade" — **verified** · +6.5% from the 06-11
