# Wolfe Research opens SMCI coverage on a risk-heavy view (SMCI)

## Summary
Wolfe Research's George Notter (a top-3% analyst) initiated [[SMCI]] at a neutral rating
tier (Peer Perform, no price target): the AI-infrastructure tailwind is real, but the
risks are stacked — rising customer concentration as CoreWeave [[CRWV]] and xAI scale,
margin-mix pressure, and a governance/regulatory overhang led by the DOJ's indictment of
co-founder Wally Liaw. Offsetting positives: record-high backlog and an intact NVIDIA
relationship. The clip also bundled a second, bullish SMCI op-ed (separate author) whose
hard financials I verified and routed where they stand on their own. Source was an
Obsidian Web Clip (TipRanks is Cloudflare-walled); the raw clip is in
`_inbox/2026-06-11 smci-wolfe-risks.clip.md`. Price baseline is the 2026-06-11 close.
