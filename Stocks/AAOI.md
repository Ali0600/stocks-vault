## What's Unique
One of the few vertically integrated, US-listed transceiver pure plays — it designs
its own lasers and builds modules in-house, so it captures the hyperscaler
800G→1.6T upgrade cycle without depending on merchant components.

### Only They Do
- In-house laser fabs (Sugar Land, TX) feeding its own modules — one of the only non-Chinese laser-die-to-module vertically integrated makers, which matters as hyperscalers diversify away from China-concentrated optics supply
- Among US-listed pure plays, first to volume-ship 800G to a hyperscaler (1.6T engagement underway per CEO)

**Chokepoint:** Medium — transceivers are multi-sourced (Innolight leads global share), but non-Chinese vertically integrated capacity is scarce and demand currently outruns it.
