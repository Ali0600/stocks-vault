## What's Unique
Lead asset AlloNK is an off-the-shelf (allogeneic) NK-cell therapy — no patient-specific cell manufacturing, unlike CAR-T — being steered from cancer toward autoimmune diseases like rheumatoid arthritis and lupus.

### Only They Do
