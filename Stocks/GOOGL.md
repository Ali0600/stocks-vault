## What's Unique
One of the few players that owns the full AI stack end-to-end — frontier models (Gemini), its own training/inference silicon ([[Custom AI silicon|TPUs]], co-designed with [[AVGO]]), a hyperscale cloud (GCP), and the Search/ads distribution that funds it all.

### Only They Do
- A vertically integrated AI stack: custom [[Custom AI silicon|TPU]] silicon (7+ generations, co-designed with [[AVGO]], fabbed at TSMC) powering both Google Cloud and internal training — cutting dependence on merchant GPUs — alongside Gemini and global Search/ads distribution
- **Chokepoint:** Low–Medium — an enormous platform and one of the few with captive AI silicon, but the TPUs are largely captive (not sold merchant) and GCP competes with larger clouds; a commercial moat, not a physical gate on others
