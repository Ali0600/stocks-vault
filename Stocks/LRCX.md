## What's Unique
A co-leader (with [[AMAT]]) in etch and deposition — the steps that build 3D NAND stacks and gate-all-around transistors.

### Only They Do
- One of only a handful of credible etch & deposition suppliers; its tools are effectively required to build today's 3D and gate-all-around device structures
- **Chokepoint:** High — part of the five-vendor [[Wafer-fab equipment|WFE]] oligopoly (~56–66% combined share); fabs can't ramp without its etch/deposition, though it shares the layer with [[AMAT]] and Tokyo Electron
