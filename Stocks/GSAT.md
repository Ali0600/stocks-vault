# Globalstar, Inc. (GSAT)

## Overview
Globalstar runs a low-earth-orbit satellite network and licensed spectrum, anchored by a multi-year direct-to-device deal with a major smartphone maker (Apple's emergency SOS / messaging) — the most established direct-to-device peer to ASTS's direct-to-cell model. ~$10B market cap. Watchlist note: Frontier Tech research, no position.

## What's Unique
Owns LEO satellites *and* terrestrial-usable spectrum, with an anchor-tenant smartphone partnership funding the network — a contracted, cash-flowed path into direct-to-device that most space-comms startups lack.

### Only They Do
- A direct-to-device satellite network underwritten by a major-smartphone anchor tenant (Apple emergency messaging) plus licensed Band 53 spectrum
- **Chokepoint:** Low — a real spectrum + anchor-customer position, but direct-to-device is becoming crowded (Starlink, [[ASTS]]); not a gate
