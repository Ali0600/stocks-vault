# GE Vernova Inc. (GEV)

## Overview
GE Vernova is the energy business spun out of GE (2024): gas/nuclear/hydro/steam power generation (Power), wind turbines (Wind), and grid electrification — transformers, switchgear, grid systems (Electrification). A core supplier of the generation and grid equipment the AI data-center buildout increasingly depends on. ~$253B market cap. Watchlist note: AI-supply-chain (power layer) research, no position.

## What's Unique
One of a handful of vertically scaled makers of both grid-scale power generation (gas/nuclear/hydro turbines) and the grid electrification gear — transformers, switchgear — whose multi-year lead times have become a binding constraint on energizing new data centers ([[Datacenter power]]).

### Only They Do
- Scaled supply of large grid transformers, switchgear, and gas/nuclear turbines together — the [[Datacenter power|grid-electrification]] equipment whose long lead times gate when new datacenter capacity can actually be energized
- **Chokepoint:** Medium — large-power-transformer and grid-equipment capacity is genuinely constrained (multi-year lead times, few large makers), gating datacenter energization; but GEV is one of several global suppliers (Siemens Energy, Hitachi Energy, ABB), not sole-source
