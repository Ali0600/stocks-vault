## What's Unique
Large phased-array BlueBird satellites aim to deliver true broadband — not just text — directly to ordinary unmodified smartphones, sold through carrier partnerships rather than as a separate device or plan.

### Only They Do
- Large phased-array satellite design (substantial patent portfolio) enabling broadband — not just text — direct to ordinary unmodified smartphones
- **Chokepoint:** Medium (its niche) / None (AI infra) — the patent estate is a real barrier in direct-to-device, but this is a space/telco thesis, unconnected to the AI build-out; Starlink Direct-to-Cell is the looming rival

## Competitors
- SpaceX Starlink Direct-to-Cell (private) — the dominant rival, currently text-first
- Globalstar (GSAT) — powers Apple's emergency SOS
- Lynk Global (private) — direct-to-phone, narrowband
- Iridium (IRDM) — established satellite messaging
