# Centrus Energy Corp. (LEU)

## Overview
Centrus is the only US-based, NRC-licensed producer of HALEU (high-assay low-enriched uranium) — the fuel most advanced reactors (Oklo, microreactors) require but which the West has almost no supply of. It also sells conventional LEU and technical services. A genuine domestic fuel bottleneck for the whole advanced-nuclear theme. ~$3.4B market cap. Watchlist note: Frontier Tech research, no position.

## What's Unique
The only operating US-licensed HALEU enrichment capability — the fuel that advanced reactors need and that, outside Russia, barely exists. A strategic, government-backed domestic chokepoint at the front of the advanced-nuclear fuel cycle.

### Only They Do
- The sole NRC-licensed US producer of HALEU (high-assay low-enriched uranium), with domestic centrifuge technology — strategic de-risking from Russian (Rosatom) supply
- **Chokepoint:** Medium — HALEU is a real, scarce gate on advanced-reactor deployment and Centrus is the only Western producer scaling it; but capacity is ramping and other enrichers (Urenco, Orano) could enter
