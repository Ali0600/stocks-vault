# NVIDIA Corporation (NVDA)

## Overview
NVIDIA designs the GPUs, networking silicon and rack-scale systems that run most of the world's AI training and inference. It is the largest company in this portfolio (~$4.9T market cap) and still its profit engine: ~63% net margin with revenue up ~85% year over year at last report.

## What's Unique
The [[CUDA]] software ecosystem locks in developers, and NVIDIA sells the whole AI compute platform — GPU plus NVLink/Mellanox networking plus rack-scale systems — not just a chip.

### Only They Do
- CUDA — a proprietary 15+-year software/developer ecosystem rivals can't legally clone and that locks AI workloads onto NVIDIA hardware
- [[GPU interconnect|NVLink/NVSwitch]] GPU-to-GPU interconnect and integrated rack-scale (GB-class) systems
- **Chokepoint:** High — CUDA lock-in plus supply allocation give real pricing power, but AMD's ROCm and hyperscaler custom silicon ([[MRVL]]/Broadcom-built TPU/Trainium) are genuine escape routes, so not Extreme
