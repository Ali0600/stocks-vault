## What's Unique
EDA software (digital/custom IC design + verification) used to design essentially every advanced chip, paired with the broadest silicon interface-IP portfolio (PCIe, HBM, USB, Die-to-Die) — a tools-and-IP position that compounds across the whole semiconductor industry ([[EDA & chip IP]]).

### Only They Do
- One of two EDA vendors (with [[CDNS]]) whose tools are required to design and verify essentially every advanced chip, plus the #1 silicon interface-IP library
- **Chokepoint:** High — the EDA duopoly gates all chip design and switching vendors mid-design is impractical; not Extreme because [[CDNS]] is a full second source (and Siemens EDA a third)
