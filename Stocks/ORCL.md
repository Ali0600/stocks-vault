## What's Unique
A mission-critical enterprise-database install base (deep switching costs) funding an aggressive OCI build-out — a hyperscaler cloud differentiated by a Bring-Your-Own-Hardware model (customers buy their own GPUs while Oracle runs the infrastructure at no margin erosion) and one of tech's largest contracted backlogs.

### Only They Do
- One of the largest AI-cloud contracted backlogs in tech — $638B RPO — plus a Bring-Your-Own-Hardware (BYOH) model that lets customers fund the GPUs while Oracle supplies the cloud/infrastructure without margin erosion
- **Chokepoint:** Low–Medium — the Oracle-database install base is a genuine lock-in, but OCI is AI-cloud capacity competing with larger, better-capitalized hyperscalers ([[MSFT]], AWS, Google) and is itself capacity- and funding-constrained, not a gate on others
