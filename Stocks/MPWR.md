## What's Unique
High-performance DC-DC power-management ICs and integrated power modules — the voltage-regulation (VRM) and power-stage silicon that feeds clean, dense current to GPUs and CPUs, an increasingly hard problem at AI-accelerator power levels.

### Only They Do
- High-density DC-DC power-management ICs / power stages designed into AI-accelerator and server power delivery — the VRM layer between the board and the GPU
- **Chokepoint:** Medium — GPU/board power-delivery design wins are sticky and technically demanding, but multi-sourced (Infineon, TI, Renesas)
