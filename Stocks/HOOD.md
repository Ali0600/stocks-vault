# Robinhood Markets, Inc. (HOOD)

## Overview
Robinhood has evolved from meme-trading app to diversified retail-finance platform — options, crypto, retirement accounts, and now prediction markets, which current coverage calls a "hot, lucrative market" entry. ~$78B market cap.

## What's Unique
The mobile-first, commission-free design that defined modern retail trading — now bundling equities, options, crypto, retirement, and prediction markets into a single consumer app with the brand's grip on younger investors.

### Only They Do
- Nothing structurally exclusive — the mobile-first, commission-free design it pioneered has been copied by every incumbent; its edge is brand and product velocity with younger investors
- **Chokepoint:** None — no proprietary barrier; competes on UX and price

## Competitors
- Charles Schwab (SCHW) — incumbent brokerage scale
- Coinbase (COIN) — crypto-trading overlap
- Interactive Brokers (IBKR) — active-trader platform
- Webull / SoFi (BULL/SOFI) — direct neobroker rivals
