## What's Unique
The mobile-first, commission-free design that defined modern retail trading — now bundling equities, options, crypto, retirement, and prediction markets into a single consumer app with the brand's grip on younger investors.

### Only They Do
- Nothing structurally exclusive — the mobile-first, commission-free design it pioneered has been copied by every incumbent; its edge is brand and product velocity with younger investors
- **Chokepoint:** None — no proprietary barrier; competes on UX and price
