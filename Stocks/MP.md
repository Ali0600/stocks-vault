# MP Materials Corp. (MP)

## Overview
MP Materials operates Mountain Pass, the only scaled US rare-earth mine, and is integrating downstream into magnets — a strategic-asset story (DoD and Apple agreements in 2025) wrapped in a commodity stock. ~$9.5B market cap.

## What's Unique
Owns Mountain Pass, the only scaled US rare-earth mine, and is integrating downstream into finished magnets — a domestic strategic-supply asset (DoD and Apple agreements) that policy actively wants to exist, unlike a pure commodity miner.

### Only They Do
- Operates Mountain Pass — the only scaled rare-earth mine in the US — and is the only domestic player integrating into finished NdFeB [[Rare-earth magnets|magnets]]
- **Chokepoint:** High (Western supply) / Low (global) — genuinely sole US source, so it gates any "de-risk from China" supply chain (defense, EV, robotics motors); globally China still sets price and volume, so it's a strategic chokepoint, not a market one

## Competitors
- Lynas Rare Earths (LYC.AX) — largest producer outside China
- USA Rare Earth (USAR) — domestic magnet aspirant
- Energy Fuels (UUUU) — diversifying into rare earths
- China Northern Rare Earth (state-owned) — sets the global price

## Supply Chain
### Suppliers
- Mining inputs & reagents (external)

### Customers
- Magnet/motor makers, automakers, defense primes (external — e.g. GM, Apple, DoD); tangential to AI compute

## Facts
- 2026-06-13 — China controls ~85–90% of global NdFeB magnet manufacturing (Bloomberg Intelligence projects ~69% by 2030); 2025 Chinese export controls now gate defense-grade (Dy/Tb) magnets — the strategic backdrop for MP as the scaled US source (reported) ([[Rare-earth magnets]])
