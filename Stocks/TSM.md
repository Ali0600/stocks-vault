# Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company Limited (TSM)

## Overview
Taiwan Semiconductor manufactures the leading-edge chips nearly everyone else in this portfolio designs — the closest thing the AI build-out has to a toll booth. ~$2.1T market cap, ~47% net margin.

## What's Unique
The dominant leading-edge pure-play foundry (3nm/2nm) with a yield and ecosystem lead nobody has matched — a neutral toll booth almost every AI chip designer in this portfolio is built around, which is also its concentration risk.

### Only They Do
- The only foundry reliably high-volume-manufacturing [[Foundry process nodes|leading-edge logic]] (3nm now, 2nm ramping) for *external* customers — Samsung trails on yield, [[INTC]] is not yet a merchant option at parity
- [[CoWoS]] advanced packaging (interposer/substrate) — the capacity AI accelerators physically require, which TSMC has been the gating supplier for industry-wide
- **Chokepoint:** Extreme — nearly every AI chip in this portfolio ([[NVDA]], [[MRVL]], AMD) is fabbed and CoWoS-packaged here; its capacity is the single hardest gate in the whole chain, and a Taiwan geographic concentration risk on top

## Competitors
- [[INTC]] — foundry challenger (18A)
- Samsung (005930.KS) — the only other leading-edge foundry
- GlobalFoundries (GFS) — trailing-edge specialist
- UMC (UMC) — mature-node foundry

## Supply Chain
### Suppliers
- [[AMAT]] · [[ASML]] · [[LRCX]] · [[KLAC]] — fab equipment ([[ASML]]'s EUV is the sole-source gate; + ext Tokyo Electron)

### Customers
- [[NVDA]] · [[MRVL]] · [[AVGO]] · [[AMD]] — fabs + CoWoS packaging (+ ext Apple)

## Facts
- 2026-06-13 — CoWoS advanced packaging is sold out through 2026 and oversubscribed — the single tightest gate in the AI stack; NVIDIA alone has reportedly booked >50% of 2026 CoWoS capacity (reported) ([[CoWoS]])
- 2026-06-13 — N2 (2nm) entered volume production in Q4 2025 — TSMC's first gate-all-around node, ~15% denser and ~25–30% lower power than N3, with the best yields of the three leading-edge fabs ([[Foundry process nodes]])
