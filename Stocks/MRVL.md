# Marvell Technology, Inc. (MRVL)

## Overview
Marvell supplies custom AI silicon (XPUs for hyperscalers) and the electro-optics that connect AI clusters — a picks-and-shovels play one layer below NVIDIA. ~$221B market cap.

## What's Unique
Combines [[Custom AI silicon|custom XPU/ASIC]] design with the electro-optics (DSP, SerDes) that wire AI clusters together — the two scarce pieces hyperscalers need to build their own accelerators instead of buying merchant GPUs.

### Only They Do
- One of only two merchant providers (with Broadcom) of custom hyperscaler XPUs/ASICs at scale, paired with in-house SerDes and electro-optics/DSP IP
- **Chokepoint:** Medium — a duopoly, not sole-source, but the custom-silicon + high-speed-interconnect combination is genuinely scarce and gates hyperscalers building their own accelerators

## Competitors
- [[AVGO]] — dominant custom-ASIC rival
- [[NVDA]] — merchant-GPU alternative to custom silicon
- [[COHR]] / [[LITE]] — optics-component overlap
- Astera Labs (ALAB) — connectivity silicon

## Supply Chain
### Suppliers

### Customers
- Hyperscalers (external) — custom ASIC/XPU programs (AWS, Google, Microsoft, Meta)
- [[AAOI]] · [[COHR]] · [[LITE]] and other module makers — merchant electro-optics DSP

## Facts
- 2026-06-13 — Marvell and Broadcom together do ~95% of custom-AI-ASIC co-design (Broadcom ~70% of the custom-accelerator market) — Marvell is #2 in a duopoly serving hyperscaler in-house chips (reported) ([[Custom AI silicon]])
