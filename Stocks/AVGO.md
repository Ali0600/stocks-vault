## What's Unique
Designs the custom ASICs (XPUs) that Google (TPU), Meta (MTIA) and others use to bypass NVIDIA — ~70% of the custom-accelerator market — plus the Ethernet switch silicon (Tomahawk/Jericho) that wires AI clusters.

### Only They Do
- Co-designs ~70% of the world's custom AI accelerators (Google TPU — 7 generations since 2014, Meta MTIA, plus an OpenAI 10GW program) — the primary route around [[CUDA|NVIDIA's GPUs]] for hyperscalers ([[Custom AI silicon]])
- Tomahawk 6 (102.4 Tbps) and Jericho 4 — merchant Ethernet silicon scaling AI [[GPU interconnect|scale-out]] fabrics
- **Chokepoint:** High — with [[MRVL]] it forms the custom-ASIC duopoly (~95% of co-design), and its switch silicon anchors AI Ethernet; the single biggest structural threat to NVIDIA's merchant-GPU share
