## What's Unique
A licensing + per-chip-royalty model on the most widely used CPU architecture — its cores sit in NVIDIA's Grace, hyperscaler server CPUs (Graviton, Axion) and nearly all mobile, giving it a toll on a huge fraction of the world's compute without manufacturing a chip ([[EDA & chip IP]]).

### Only They Do
