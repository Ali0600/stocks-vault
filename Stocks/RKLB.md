# Rocket Lab Corporation (RKLB)

Still pre-profit while it scales. ~$53B market cap. Watchlist note: Frontier Tech research, no position.

## What's Unique
A two-segment model — launch (Electron, the reusable medium-lift Neutron) *and* space systems (satellite buses, star trackers, solar, components) — that lets it sell across the whole space stack rather than just rides to orbit.

### Only They Do
- One of the only Western companies with both an operational orbital launch vehicle and a scaled space-systems/components business under one roof
- **Chokepoint:** Low — launch and space hardware are capacity/execution advantages, not a gate; competes with SpaceX, ULA, and a field of space-systems vendors
