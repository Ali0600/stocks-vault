## What's Unique
The only US-based leading-edge integrated device maker — it both designs and fabricates x86 CPUs, and is standing up a foundry (18A) as a domestic alternative to TSMC, which carries strategic/government weight no fabless rival has.

### Only They Do
- The only US-headquartered company operating [[Foundry process nodes|leading-edge logic]] fabs — strategic/geographic exclusivity underpinning CHIPS-Act and government interest
- x86 architecture license (shared only with AMD)
- **Chokepoint:** Low–Medium — the exclusivity is geographic/strategic, not technical; on process it trails [[TSM]], so today it's a would-be second source rather than an active gate
