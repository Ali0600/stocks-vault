## B. Riley Analyst Is Bullish on MRVL Stock

Ellis believes that the joint CEO appearance at the Computex event indicates that Nvidia’s partnership with Marvell has expanded quickly. Notably, the two companies are collaborating on custom AI chips, NVLink Fusion, as well as Celestial and in-house optics. Moreover, the 5-star analyst noted that Huang’s statement that Marvell is “ [the next $1 trillion company](https://www.tipranks.com/news/marvell-stock-mrvl-soars-25-after-nvidia-ceo-jensen-huang-calls-it-next-trillion-dollar-company) ” bodes well for its expanded SAM (serviceable addressable market) revenue conversion.

Secondly, Ellis views Marvell’s S&P 500 inclusion on June 22 favorably and expects it to expand MRVL’s ownership base, both directly and indirectly.

Finally, Ellis reacted positively to Durn’s appointment as Marvell’s new CFO. The analyst highlighted that Durn’s appointment as CFO follows his two years as a director on the company’s board. He also noted Durn’s 30-plus years of experience, including CFO roles at Adobe [ADBE -6.76% ▼](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/adbe) , Applied Materials [AMAT +2.64% ▲](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/amat) , NXP [NXPI +0.76% ▲](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/nxpi) , Freescale Semiconductor [(FSL)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/fsl), and GlobalFoundries [GFS +0.79% ▲](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/gfs) . Ellis added that his interaction with Durn at a prior role at Applied Materials reflected that he is a “strong financial leader with a clear strategic focus and strong operational grasp,” which the analyst believes could drive MRVL stock higher over the long term.

## What’s Behind the ADBE Stock Price Target Cuts?

[Stifel Nicolaus](https://www.tipranks.com/experts/firms/stifel-nicolaus) analyst [J. Parker Lane](https://www.tipranks.com/experts/analysts/j.-parker-lane) is among the analysts who lowered their price targets for ADBE stock. He reduced his price target for the shares to $200 from $350. This new price target represents a possible 1.72% downside for the shares. On that same note, the analyst also downgraded Adobe stock to a Hold rating from a Buy rating.

Lane took issue with several pieces of news in the Adobe earnings report. This included the company’s lowered organic ARR outlook for the second half of the year. He argued that Adobe traded short-term ARR growth for monthly active user growth via its push of freemium services and delay of planned price changes. Lane also noted that the departure of Adobe Chief Financial Officer Dan Durn added leadership uncertainty to the earnings report.

Here are some of the [other analyst price target cuts](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/adbe/forecast) that hit Adobe stock.

- Morgan Stanley analyst [Elizabeth Porter](https://www.tipranks.com/experts/analysts/elizabeth-porter) reduced her price target to $365 from $425.
- Wells Fargo analyst [Michael Turrin](https://www.tipranks.com/experts/analysts/michael-turrin) cut his price target to $250 from $330.
- [RBC Capital](https://www.tipranks.com/experts/firms/rbc-capital) analyst [Matthew Swanson](https://www.tipranks.com/experts/analysts/matthew-swanson) lowered his price target to $285 from $350.
- Robert W. Baird analyst [Robert Oliver](https://www.tipranks.com/experts/analysts/robert-oliver) slashed his price target to $230 from $270.
